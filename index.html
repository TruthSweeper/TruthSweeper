How To Play

Click on squares on the grid to see each question

Each question has 4 options, with one being the correct answer

Decide whether the option highlighted in green is correct and then either

Mark the square as correct

Mark the square as incorrect

The red quares show how many adjacent squares are incorrect

Once all squares have been marked you may finish the game and see your score
